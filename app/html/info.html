About EasyRunes

EasyRunes uses statistics from KoreanBuilds and data from Riot API.
KoreanBuilds data is obtained from match history from summoners in Korea that are Master and above. Some champions do not have available runes page on them because there are insufficient data that can be calculated reliably. More info can be found on KoreanBuilds FAQ.

How to Use

  1. Launch EasyRunes and pick a champion in Champ Select.
  2. Runes will be fetched and loaded for you.
  3. Click on the upload button to upload the runes to your client.
